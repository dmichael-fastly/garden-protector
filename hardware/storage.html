Photo storage & cleanup

Your cameras tuck every sighting away safely in the cloud. Saving photos less often, or at a smaller size, keeps fewer and lighter photos around — and a smaller bill. Older photos are tidied out automatically on the schedule you pick.

Connect your garden to the cloud first (over in Settings) before automatic cleanups can run.

Saving photos to the cloud

Saving less often or smaller means fewer, lighter photos in the cloud. “After dark” skips the routine night photos (your cameras can’t see much in the dark anyway) — leave it on “when something moves” to still catch a late-night visitor where there’s some porch light. Your live view is always full quality.
