Set up your Garden Node

The Garden Node is the little battery-powered box that lives out in the garden — it watches for visitors, reads the weather (temperature, humidity, rain), and switches on the spray when something needs shooing off.

What you'll need:

  • The node board and its radar sensor
  • A spray gadget (pump + nozzle) and its power
  • A few minutes to get it connected
